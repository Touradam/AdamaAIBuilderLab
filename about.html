About AI Education Program

Build tools that optimize your life — don't let AI use you. We teach anyone to understand AI, think critically, and build real applications with intention.

Our Mission

We are launching a 4-week live AI education program (3 sessions per week) designed for anyone — no prior technical background required.

We teach people how to understand AI, think critically about it, and use it to build real tools that improve their lives — without becoming dependent, manipulated, or replaced by it.

I believe AI is the most powerful tool humanity has ever created. Those who learn how to use it intentionally and responsibly will thrive in the coming years. Those who don't risk becoming passive users — or victims of it.

This program focuses on understanding what AI really is, how machine learning works, gaining practical skills to build real applications, and developing AI literacy, agency, and ethical awareness. Week 1 is free so you can experience real wins — a live website and GitHub setup — before committing to the full program.

What Sets Us Apart

No Prerequisites Required

You don't need any prior programming or AI experience. We start from the fundamentals and build up week by week, ensuring everyone can follow along.

Week 1 Free — Try Before You Pay

Start with a free trial week. Build and deploy a real website, set up GitHub, and gain AI coding experience before deciding to continue.

Understand First, Build With Purpose

We combine critical thinking about AI with hands-on building — so you use AI deliberately, not passively, while shipping real projects from Session 1.

3 Live Sessions Per Week

12 hands-on sessions over 4 weeks — Friday 7–8 PM, Saturday & Sunday 8–10 AM (PST). Every week ends with something you can show: a live site, extension, AI app, or capstone on Demo Day.

Real Outcomes

By graduation you'll have a deployed website, GitHub profile, Chrome extension, AI web app, and bonus resources: prompt templates, Git cheat sheets, and deployment checklists.

Who Should Join

This program is designed for anyone who uses a computer in their work and wants to stay relevant in a world shaped by AI. No prior programming or AI experience is required.

It is especially valuable for recent graduates struggling to enter the job market, as it builds practical, in-demand skills to become a stronger candidate—or to start building independent online work and businesses.

About the Instructor

Adama Touré

Adama Touré is a renewable energy engineer, AI builder, entrepreneur, and systems thinker passionate about helping people use technology intentionally to improve their lives and communities.

Adama brings an interdisciplinary background spanning engineering, architecture, physics, mathematics, artificial intelligence, and startup development. His work combines technical depth with a systems-level approach to solving real-world problems.

He is the founder of Solar Sense, an AI-powered solar monitoring technology designed to make solar energy systems safer, smarter, and more resilient through real-time sensing and machine learning fault detection. His research has also explored second-life solar panel reuse, solar thermal energy systems, and sustainable infrastructure for underserved regions.
